# Product Rules

## Product Identity
El Rincón Monterizo is an on-demand digital platform for educational, cultural, and audiovisual content.

## Core Product Principles
- content should feel organized and valuable
- the experience should be free and accessible in the initial stage
- advertising must be clean and non-invasive
- the platform should represent community and cultural value
- the user experience should prioritize clarity over overload

## Current Content Types
The platform is expected to support:
- digital books
- documents and educational materials
- on-demand videos
- cultural and community content
- sponsor placements

## Monetization Rules
Initial monetization is based on:
- local sponsor blocks
- clean banner placements
- featured local businesses
- non-invasive promotional spaces

The initial MVP does not depend on:
- paid subscriptions
- paywalls
- premium-only plans

## Streaming Rules
- the product is on-demand only
- live streaming is not part of the current scope

## Advertising Rules
Advertising should:
- integrate naturally with the platform
- not damage readability or trust
- not dominate the content experience
- be visually consistent with the design

Avoid:
- aggressive popups
- autoplay ad experiences
- cluttered layouts
- excessive ad density

## Content Rules
All content published must be:
- owned by the project, or
- properly licensed, or
- explicitly authorized for use/distribution

Do not assume that third-party content can be published without permission.

## UX Rules
The user experience must aim for:
- simple navigation
- readable structure
- clear content hierarchy
- minimal friction
- responsive behavior

## MVP Rules
The MVP should stay focused on:
- clear homepage
- content discovery
- content detail pages
- sponsor/ad placements
- future-ready structure

Avoid overloading the MVP with secondary features.

## Growth Rules
Future features should only be added if they support:
- real user value
- product clarity
- business sustainability
- maintainable architecture
